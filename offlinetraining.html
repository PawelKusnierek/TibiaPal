This tool calculates the time it will take you to reach your target skill while offline or online training.

If you select 'offline' training, the tool assumes full 12h of offline training per day.

The tool assumes:
- Magic level training for mages (Druids & Sorcerers)
- Melee (Axe/Club/Sword) training for Knights
- Distance training for Paladins
- Fist training for Monks
