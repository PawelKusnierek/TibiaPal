Wheel of Destiny Presets

  1. The presets you will find here only cover the key upgrades/breakpoints of the wheel system, as we cannot have a preset for every level.
    If your level is between any two presets, we suggest to simply aim for simple stuff such as +life/resistances, until your next big breakpoint.
  2. If you have a suggestion, or a preset that you use that isn't below, please let us know!
    Reach out to 'Kusnier' on discord or join our server to discuss, discord.gg/f2hESCbgvz
  3. We focus on presets for up to level 1000~, as at that point there are simply too many combinations and by then you should already have enough knowledge/experience to customize your own wheel.
