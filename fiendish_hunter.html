Fiendish Hunter


PURPOSE:Help in finding fiend monsters based on exiva spell outputs.
  1. Cast exiva in game.
  2. Choose place on map where spell was casted by doing right-click, or choose from dropdown list. You can search list by typing in field.
  3. Either use buttons to choose what spell is pointing to, or paste exiva output.
  4. Click Add Exiva Point to add data to map. Possible area where fiendish is present will be displayed on map, and table bellow map will be updated with data of added spell.
  5. When another spell is added, map is updated to reflect combined area of spells (if applicable).
  6. Combined area is calculated for spells with same monster difficulty level. Areas with most overlaps is highlighted. In case multiple areas have same number of overlaps, all are highlighted.
  7. To move around map use drag-drop and mouse scroll. Choose floor of map using floor buttons on left side of the map.
  8. Monster difficulty is colorcoded on map and in table.
