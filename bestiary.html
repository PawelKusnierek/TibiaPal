Tibia Bestiary Guide

  1. All Bestiary monsters can be found in one of the sections of this guide. If you are looking for a particular monster, expand all sections and use 'ctrl + f'
  2. Bestiary difficulty is roughly defined as:
    Easy - under 2h
    Medium - 2h to 6h
    Hard - over 6h
  3. The difficulty and speed of completion will vary depending on the character's level. For example, a level 1000 will of course complete bestiaries faster than a level 200.
    The difficulty rating is supposed to reflect an 'average' leveled player for the monster
  4. If you have suggestions for this guide please email me at tibiapalcontact@gmail.com.
