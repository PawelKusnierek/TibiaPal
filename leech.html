Leech formula is as follows:

100% Leech for first monster hit, additional 10% for each subsequent monster

For example, if you hit 1 monster, you will Leech 100% (damage * leech percentage), when you hit 6 monsters, you will Leech 150% (100% + 5 * 10%)
