PURPOSE: Simple reference to help decide which items are best sold on the market vs. directly to NPCs.

High value items should always be sold on the market.
Medium value items should generally be sold on the market, unless you need money immediately.
Low value items can be safely sold to NPCs, unless the supply is really low on your server.
