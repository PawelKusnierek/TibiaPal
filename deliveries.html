PURPOSE: All Weekly delivery items list. Can help in deciding which items are best sold on the market vs. safely directly to NPCs.

Very High value items should always be sold on the market.
High value items should always be sold on the market, unless you desperately need money fast.
Medium items should generally be sold on the market, but usually not for much more than the NPC price.
Low value items can be safely sold to NPCs, unless the supply is really low on your server.
