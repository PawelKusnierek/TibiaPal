Bosses in this section only appear during announced raids. Some of them are very weak, some are more tough.

IMPORTANT: when attempting to kill bosses with low health (e.g. Willi Wasp, Zomba) you should 'tag' the boss only ONCE, either with a weak rune (e.g. a poison/fire bomb) or preferably using wand/rod obtainable from Thais Museum (plain mayhem rod, valuable carving wand).

This is so other people on your server can also get the boss kill contribution.
People who one-shot/UE/SD/Avalanche etc. raid bosses like this are extremely rude, do NOT be like them.
